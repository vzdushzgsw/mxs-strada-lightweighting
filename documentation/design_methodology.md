# Design Methodology & Process Validation

### 🛠️ Technical Stack & CAD Control
*   **CAD Platform:** CATIA V5/V6 (Parametric Solid & Surface Modeling)
*   **Engineering Principles:** Geometric Dimensioning & Tolerancing (GD&T), Rib/Grid Reinforcement Architecture, Draft Angle Analysis for Injection Molding.

### 📈 Optimization Workflow
1.  **Enclosure Boundary Definition:** Mapping external hardpoints from the official AiM Strada CAD chassis.
2.  **Internal Packaging Allocation:** Modeling the custom dummy PCB clearances and mounting boss locations.
3.  **Parametric Wall-Thickness Mitigation:** Reducing nominal wall thickness and introducing localized ribbing grids to compensate for the lower modulus of elasticity in the candidate plastics.
